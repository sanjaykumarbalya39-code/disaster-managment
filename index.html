Frameworks & Certifications

TIES — The International Ecotourism Society
All destination partners meet TIES standards: minimizing environmental impact, building environmental & cultural awareness, providing positive experiences for visitors and hosts, and directing financial benefits to conservation.
ISO 14001 — Environmental Management
Our operations and all partner lodges comply with ISO 14001 standards for environmental management systems — covering energy use, waste reduction, water conservation, and biodiversity impact.
Rainforest Alliance — Sustainable Tourism
Rainforest Alliance-certified tour operators ensure fair wages and working conditions for local staff, community benefit sharing, and measurable biodiversity protection in all destinations.
Green Globe — Eco-Accommodation Standard
Eco-lodges and accommodation partners meet Green Globe's rigorous criteria for water conservation, renewable energy use, waste management, and local community engagement.
IUCN — Protected Area Guidelines
All tours operating in or near protected areas follow IUCN guidelines for visitor impact management, wildlife disturbance minimization, and habitat conservation protocols.
Carbon Neutral — Verified Offset Programs
Travel emissions are calculated, minimized, and offset through verified reforestation projects. Every booking automatically plants 10 trees through our partnered reforestation initiatives.

FAQ

Common Traveler Questions

Everything you need to know before booking your eco-tourism adventure with EcoVoyage.

What makes Stackly-Eco different from a regular travel agency?
Every experience we offer is vetted against TIES eco-tourism standards. We partner exclusively with local community operators, certified naturalist guides, and conservation organizations. A verified portion of every booking — typically 30–60% — goes directly to conservation and community projects at the destination.
Are your tours suitable for first-time eco-travelers?
Absolutely. Our Explorer packages are designed for travelers new to conservation travel — shorter itineraries, comfortable eco-lodges, and naturalist guides who tailor experiences to your comfort level. We also offer pre-trip briefings so you're fully prepared before you go.
How are carbon emissions handled for flights?
We calculate the full carbon footprint of your journey including flights, ground transport, and accommodation. You receive a carbon offset certificate for verified reforestation projects that sequester equivalent CO₂. Every booking also automatically plants 10 trees through our partnered programs.
How small are your tour groups?
Most tours have a maximum of 8–12 participants to minimize ecological impact and ensure quality naturalist guidance. Some specialist expedition programs are limited to 4–6 people. We never exceed what conservation guidelines recommend for each specific ecosystem.
Can I customize my itinerary or combine destinations?
Yes — our Conservationist and Expedition packages both offer multi-destination experiences, and our travel experts can build fully custom itineraries. Contact us with your interests (wildlife, marine, cultural, birding) and we'll design a journey that matches your passion and timeline.
