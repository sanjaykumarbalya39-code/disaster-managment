FAQ

Pricing & Procurement Questions

Common questions from finance officers, procurement teams, and agency administrators.

What is included in the per-person price?
All packages are fully all-inclusive. The per-person price covers: eco-lodge accommodation, all meals, expert naturalist guide, local ground transport, entrance fees to parks and reserves, and your carbon offset program. International flights are included only in the Expedition package; Explorer and Conservationist prices cover in-country travel only.
Can I book for two people or a small private group?
Absolutely. Couples, friends, and small private groups (2–8 people) can book any package as a private group experience — prices remain the same per person. For groups of 9 or more, contact us for tailored group pricing and itinerary design.
Is there a deposit or payment plan available?
A 30% deposit secures your booking, with the balance due 60 days before departure. Flexible payment plans spreading the balance across monthly installments are available — ask our team when enquiring.
What is your cancellation and refund policy?
Cancellations made 90+ days before departure receive a full refund minus a small processing fee. Cancellations within 60–89 days retain 50% refund. Within 60 days, deposits are non-refundable, but we offer trip rescheduling at no extra cost. We recommend purchasing travel insurance for full coverage.
Can I customize the itinerary or dates for my package?
Yes — all three packages can be tailored for your preferred dates (subject to availability) and specific interests. The Conservationist and Expedition tiers include a complimentary itinerary customization consultation with one of our conservation travel experts.
