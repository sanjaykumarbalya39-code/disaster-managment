FAQ

Pricing & Procurement Questions

Common questions from finance officers, procurement teams, and agency administrators.

Are plans available on government purchase orders or GSA schedule?
Yes. We accept government purchase orders, inter-agency transfers, and emergency procurement authorizations. GSA schedule pricing is available for US Federal and State agencies. Contact our procurement desk for documentation.
Can we upgrade plans mid-year during an active incident?
Absolutely. Plan upgrades take effect immediately upon authorization — including activation of higher-tier depot access and command support resources. Billing is pro-rated for the remaining contract period.
Is equipment purchase separate from the service plan?
Yes. Service plans cover access to our deployment network, command support, training, and pre-positioning services. Equipment procurement is quoted separately based on incident type, scale, and your existing asset inventory.
Do you offer multi-year contract discounts?
Yes. Two-year agreements receive 12% off and three-year agreements receive 20% off the published plan price. Multi-year agreements also include locked-in SLA terms and annual plan reviews.
Can multiple agencies share a single plan for joint operations?
Multi-agency consortium plans are available under the National Command tier. We support inter-agency cost-sharing arrangements and joint purchasing under mutual aid agreements.

Not Sure Which Plan Is Right for Your Agency?

Our team will assess your risk profile, existing capabilities, and operational requirements — then recommend the right tier with documentation for your procurement office.
