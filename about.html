Our Journey

From First Responders to a Global Emergency Platform

From serving FEMA regional offices to coordinating international relief operations across 72 countries and every type of disaster scenario.

  1. 01 2008

    Founded in the Field

    Launched by former FEMA incident commanders and UN relief coordinators — serving state emergency management agencies with rapid-deploy field medical stations and communication systems.

  2. 2013 02

    International Expansion

    Deployed in the aftermath of Super Typhoon Hainan, establishing our first international depots in Southeast Asia and partnerships with UN-OCHA regional offices across three continents.

  3. 2018 03

    Real-Time Command Integration

    Launched our digital command platform — integrating live asset tracking, inter-agency communications, and resource allocation dashboards into every major deployment.

  4. Today 04

    72 Countries, 2.4M Lives Impacted

    Operating across 72 countries with 14 pre-positioned depot locations, a 24/7 command support center, and 500+ agency partners — from municipal first responders to national civil defense authorities.
